Roasted Butternut Squash Salad

This butternut squash salad with raisins, onions, crispy bacon, and romaine lettuce is served warm. Simple and delicious!

  • 1 medium butternut squash, halved lengthwise and seeded
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
  • 6 slices bacon
  • 1 cup sliced onion
  • ⅓ cup pecans, toasted and chopped
  • ⅓ cup raisins
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup, or to taste


Greek Couscous Salad

This hearty Greek couscous salad uses Israeli couscous. So delicious!

  • ½ cup water
  • ¼ cup chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • ½ cup pearl (Israeli) couscous
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas (garbanzo beans), rinsed and drained
  • ¼ cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • ¼ cup sliced Kalamata olives
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper


Pecan Chicken Salad

This pecan chicken salad made with chopped cooked chicken, herbs, celery, pecans, red onion, and a creamy homemade dressing is really flavorful. Serve on top of toasted croissants or butter lettuce.

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons white wine vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon Park Hill maple pepper (such as Savory Spice Shop)
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • ⅓ cup chopped toasted pecans
  • 2 tablespoons minced red onion
